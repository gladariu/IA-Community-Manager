# Project Context

## Project Overview
Social Auto PTY - AI-powered automatic content generation platform for businesses. Generates posts, flyers, captions, and campaigns specifically for restaurants, barbershops, real estate, and workshops. Monthly subscription model.

## Constraints
- Color Palette: Primary #6C63FF (purple), Secondary #FF6B6B (coral), Dark #1A1A2E, Light #F8F9FA
- Typography: Inter for body, Poppins for headings
- Style: Modern, bold, dark-themed landing page with vibrant gradients. Clean dashboard UI.
- AI Models: gpt-5.4 for text generation, gpt-image-2 for flyer/image generation
- Business types: Bienes Raíces, Abogados, Clínicas, Talleres, Iglesias, Emprendedores
- Language: Spanish (Panama market)
