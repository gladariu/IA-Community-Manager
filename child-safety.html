Child Safety Standards and CSAE Prevention Policy

SocialAuto is committed to protecting children and preventing any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

Prohibited Content

  • Any content depicting child sexual abuse or exploitation.
  • Distribution of child sexual abuse material (CSAM).
  • Grooming, trafficking, exploitation, or harmful behavior toward minors.
  • Any activity that endangers children.

Reporting

Users may report violations by contacting:

Email: contacto@nitidoservices.com

Enforcement

  • Removal of content.
  • Suspension of accounts.
  • Termination of services.
  • Reporting to law enforcement when required.

Cooperation

SocialAuto cooperates with relevant authorities and child protection organizations to combat CSAE.
